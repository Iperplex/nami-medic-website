About Company

Building Trusted Partnerships
Within Healthcare

Established in 2022 and incorporated as Nami Medic Supplies Sdn Bhd in 2023, we are a Johor-based medical device distributor and surgical solutions provider supporting gynaecology, general surgery, and orthopaedic procedures, alongside the supply of operating theatre products and equipment for hospitals across Johor.

We currently focus on supporting hospitals and healthcare professionals across Johor through responsive service, reliable coordination, and dependable operating theatre support, while continuously building long-term professional relationships within the healthcare industry.
